quantum gravity

The project to come up with a unified scientific framework that combines quantum mechanics with general relativity, while being consistent with observed facts about the universe. In other words a framework that is viable for a theory fundamental physics.

Principles

While it is difficult to speak of principles for phenomena out of experimental reach, one thing that does seem pretty solid about quantum gravity are inferences based on thermodynamics. Considerations about black hole thermodynamics indicate that the Information should be extensive not with the full volume of space but with the area of a bounding surface. Taking this inference seriously – holography – is a major theme of quantum gravity.

  • holography

    Holography, or the holographic principle, is the idea that the Information-content of the correct theory of quantum gravity should be area-extensive rather than volume-extensive. In other words, it should be proportional to the surface area of the boundary of the region in question.

  • extensive

    Definition. An extensive property is one that combines additively when combining physical subsystems. For example, mass or entropy (at least ignoring quantum gravity). It is contrasted with intensive properties.
