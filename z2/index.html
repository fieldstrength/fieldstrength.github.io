Background

My approach to life involves looking for very general principles, and incorporating them into how I understand and act. Thus its very useful to me to have this repository as rough map of how concepts fit together. It allows me to make sure I learn new things deeply by incorporating them into the proper place in this hierarchy. And it allows me to refactor my understanding if I first learned something as a specific case before I was ready to recognize it as a manifestation of a broader pattern.

Major themes of my interests often involve ideas from fundamental physics, like unification, symmetry, duality, as well as ideas from computation and mathematics like Curry-Howard correspondence, functional programming, category theory etc.

The purpose of abstraction is not to be vague, but to create a new semantic level in which one can be absolutely precise. -Edsger W. Dijkstra
