differentiable manifold

Definition. A differentiable manifold is a topological manifold \((X,\mathcal{T})\) such that for any pair of intersecting charts \(U_i \cap U_j \neq \varnothing\), the transition functions between the charts

$$ \psi_{ij} \equiv \phi_i \circ \phi_j^{-1} $$

are smooth.
